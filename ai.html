What is Artificial intelligence

Artificial intelligence (AI) is the simulation of human intelligence processes by machines, particularly computer systems. These processes include learning, reasoning, and self-correction. Specific applications of Artificial intelligence include expert systems, speech recognition and machine vision.

AI makes it possible for machines to learn from experience, fine-tune to new inputs and perform human-like tasks. Most AI examples that you overhear about today – from chess-playing computers to self-driving cars – depend heavily on deep learning and natural language processing (NLP). Using these technologies, computers can be trained to achieve specific tasks by processing huge amounts of data and identifying patterns in the data.
